FR1EV Security Roblox Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

FR1EV Security may collect and store limited information needed to link a Discord account with a Roblox account.

Information we may store

This may include your Discord user ID, Roblox user ID, and Roblox username used during the linking process.

Why we use it

We use this information to provide account linking, Roblox verification, and related server features.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data. Data may be used only for bot functionality and moderation-related Roblox connection features.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the FR1EV Security support team.
